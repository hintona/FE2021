Placing An Order For Pickup
  1. Come into the store
  2. Grab an order sheet from next to the register
  3. Fill out the order sheet while enjoying some fresh cookies
  4. Bring your order sheet up to the register
  5. Pay for your order
  6. We will have your cookies by the time and date you requested*
*Please give us 24 hours to fufill your order

About Us
The Gingerbread House Bakery was founded in 1983 by a passionate baker from Germany. The original Gingerbread House Bakery ran out of her kitchen, limited to only what she could make and bake in her oven each day. Since then, it has expanded into a new building and brought on two more experienced bakers, to create the perfect baking team!
